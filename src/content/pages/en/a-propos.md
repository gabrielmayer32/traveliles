Travel-Îles is an independent magazine dedicated to exceptional travel, hospitality, dining, craftsmanship, and the landscapes that define Mauritian art de vivre.

We cover addresses that take their time: characterful hotels, private villas, chefs, artisans, artists, golf courses, preserved lagoons, and still-undiscovered territories.

Each subject favours the editorial gaze, quality of detail, and a genuine relationship with place.
