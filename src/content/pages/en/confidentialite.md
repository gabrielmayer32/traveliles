## Data collected

Travel-Îles does not collect any personal data. The site uses no registration form, traffic analytics tool, or advertising tracking pixel.

## Cookies

The site uses a single local cookie (`ti-cookies`) to store your choice regarding embedded videos. This cookie is stored in your browser and is not shared with any third party.

If you accept cookies, YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, or Instagram video players may set their own cookies. These cookies are governed by the respective privacy policies of those platforms.

## Embedded videos

Videos are hosted by third-party services (YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook, Instagram). By accepting video playback, you accept the terms of use and privacy policy of those services.

## Hosting

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect technical data (IP address, HTTP headers) for security and performance purposes. See the [Cloudflare privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Your rights

You can delete the consent cookie at any time in your browser settings. For any questions, contact us at [contact@traveliles.com](mailto:contact@traveliles.com).
